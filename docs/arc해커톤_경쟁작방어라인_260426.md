## 1. V1 — Arc-Native Fit 공격 답변

### 공격
> "QuantMesh는 1 쿼리 = 1 nano-payment 원자 결제 — Arc/Circle 인프라가 코어 루프에 깔려 있다. AlphaLoop은 거래 자체엔 Bybit·USDT를 쓰고, Arc는 ERC-8004 scoreboard에만 사용한다. **Arc 빼도 AlphaLoop 봇은 그대로 돌아가는 것 아닌가?**"

### 방어 (긴 버전)

**1줄**: "Arc isn't our payment rail — it's our agent identity & verifiable performance ledger."

**3줄**:
> "AlphaLoop uses Arc as **agent identity & verifiable performance ledger**, not payment rail. Five ERC-8004 AgentRegistered events on contract `0xb276b96f…b7ab`, each carrying a sha256 contentHash that links the on-chain event to the off-chain agent-card JSON. Tamper with the card, the hash mismatches. **Without Arc, the marketplace claim — 'these five agents really are who they say' — is unverifiable.** The settlement layer (variable-price x402) is downstream; it works on Arc because USDC-as-gas closes the loop, but the trust primitive lives on-chain."

**5줄 (인터뷰 깊이)**:
> "We took a different angle than QuantMesh's nano-payment architecture. They're optimizing for atomic per-query settlement; we're optimizing for **trust-minimized agent reputation**. Two reasons: (1) sub-cent payment without identity is a treadmill — the next bot replaces this one, no continuity. (2) ERC-8004 emits an on-chain *card hash + role + wallet* — that's the missing infrastructure for an agentic economy. Once identity is anchored, nano-payment marketplaces (QuantMesh-style) compose on top as a downstream layer. **We didn't pick the easier integration; we picked the underlying primitive.**"

### 보강 증거 (질문 후속 시)
- `https://signal-mesh.vercel.app/scoreboard.html` — 5건 AgentRegistered live verifiable
- `docs/evidence/erc8004_registry.json` — 모든 tx hash + contentHash + block number

## 2. V2 — Agentic vs Quant bot 공격 답변

### 공격
> "arc-signal-desk는 4-agent NLP swarm이다. ARCEON·Agent Prime도 multi-agent orchestration. **AlphaLoop은 9개 dual-quote 봇 — 같은 전략의 9개 인스턴스**일 뿐 multi-agent intelligence가 아니다."

### 방어 (긴 버전)

**1줄**: "Three structurally distinct lanes, not nine clones."

**3줄**:
> "Three structurally distinct lanes: **v1 kimchi premium (Korean ↔ Global, threshold 0.6%, action `OPEN_UPBIT_SHORT_BINANCE_LONG`), v2 dual-quote (intra-venue USDT/USDC, threshold = venue fee envelope, action `TRADE_DT|DC`), v3 funding-rate basis (Binance fapi /v1/premiumIndex, threshold 0.05%/8h, action `OPEN_FUNDING_LONG_SPOT_SHORT_PERP`).** Different data sources, different threshold semantics, different action verbs. The Q-learner picks among lanes by **regime** — hot-vol picks v1 (q=3.22), hot-funding picks v2, calm-cold picks v3."

**5줄 (인터뷰 깊이)**:
> "The 'multi-agent' label can mean two things. Swarm-style (4 NLP agents debating) is one — that's what arc-signal-desk does. Ours is the second: **agents with separate economic identities, separate on-chain wallets, separate ERC-8004 cards, separate PnL streams, transacting through a learned meta-policy.** Five wallets, five AgentRegistered events. The meta-agent isn't an aggregator; it's a participant — pays the producers, gets paid by the executor, and re-prices its own signals based on outcome feedback. **Honest comparison: arc-signal-desk has more agents *in conversation*; we have more agents *in business*.** Both are valid; we picked the one where the agents have skin in the game."

## 4. Ed Thorp — Kelly sizing 공격

### 공격
> "1977 USDT 풀에서 9코인 휴리스틱 분배. **Kelly fraction 명시 없음. 왜 이 사이즈?**"

### 방어
> "1/4 Kelly cap, bounded by **`min($500 paper book × 1/4 Kelly, 0.01% × 24h venue volume)`** per coin. Full Kelly on a sub-cent edge magnifies variance to where one IOC_MISS run wipes a week of PnL — 1/4 Kelly is the conservative bound. Per-coin volume cap (0.01% × 24h) handles slippage. This isn't Kelly-optimal — it's Kelly-bounded. SUBMISSION.md §13 has the formula."

### Correlation 공격
> "9 alts ~0.7-0.9 BTC correlation → 사실상 1 포지션 9배 아닌가?"

> "Two of three lanes are net-delta-neutral by construction — v2 dual-quote (intra-venue spread) and v3 funding (long spot + short perp) carry zero directional beta. Only v1 kimchi has directional KR-Global beta. **Simultaneous BTC crash compresses spreads** — that's the risk we accept, not directional drawdown across all three lanes. SUBMISSION.md §13."
